Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to the Price of Betrayal game website and its email notification sign-up form. References to "we", "us", or "our" refer to the game's developer/publisher.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit your email address via our sign-up form, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of your submission
  • Your explicit consent to be contacted

We do not collect cookies, tracking pixels, or any other personal data from your visit.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email address will only be used to notify you when Price of Betrayal launches or reaches key milestones, and to send occasional development updates. We will never sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.

4. How We Protect Your Data

Your email address is encrypted in your browser using RSA-OAEP asymmetric encryption before it is transmitted. Only the game's developer — holding the corresponding private key — can read your address. No third party, including our storage provider, has access to it in plaintext.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you provide by checking the consent box on the sign-up form.

6. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request deletion at any time (right to be forgotten)
  • Withdraw consent — opt out at any time by contacting us
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Non-discrimination (CCPA) — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address until the game launches and initial notifications have been sent, or until you request deletion — whichever comes first.

8. Contact

To exercise any of your rights, contact us at: privacy@yourgame.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the game develops. Material changes will be posted on this page.
